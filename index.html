Services

Firewood is not our only specialty at the Kennebunk Firewood Company. We also offer logging, lot clearing, snowplowing, tractor work, and yard maintenance services.

  • Firewood

    Need firewood? We cut, split, and deliver firewood to your home or location. We can also stack the wood for you at your location.

  • Land and Brush Clearing

    Do you need a few trees taken down or a whole area cut?

  • Snowplowing

    Have a driveway, parking lot, or sidewalk that you need cleared of snow. Contact us for all of your snow removal needs.

  • Landscaping

    In need of a walkways, patios, or retaining walls. Give us a call for all of your landscape or hardscape needs.

  • Tractor Work

  • Yard Services

    Is your yard in need of some brush removal? Has a recent storm left debris in your yard or are you ready for a seasonal cleanup? We also provide lawn mowing and raking services.
